GenBank vs SRA release dates

Two month period from mid-May to mid-July with no GenBank submissions, but recent GenBank submissions from early Aug match to SRA submissions from early July

Timeline of ~1 month for collection to SRA and then a ~1 month delay to GenBank submission

Ongoing Nextstrain work

  • Continuing to build out core pathogens, each with automated data ingest and analysis, including seasonal coronaviruses (live), rabies (in progress), West Nile virus (in progress), Lassa (in progress), yellow fever (in progress) and Oropouche (in progress)
  • Weighted subsampling in augur filter
  • Combining multiple metadata TSV files with augur merge
  • Combining across multiple augur filter calls into a single augur subsample call with associated YAML (analogous to ncov workflow)
  • Splash page updates to highlight relevant datasets
