Timely surveillance and rapid analysis essential to understand ongoing influenza evolution

nextflu

Project to provide a real-time view of the evolving influenza population

nextflu pipeline

  1. Download all recent HA sequences from GISAID
  2. Filter to remove outliers
  3. Subsample across time and space
  4. Align sequences
  5. Build tree
  6. Estimate frequencies
  7. Infer antigenic phenotypes
  8. Export for visualization

Up-to-date analysis publicly available at:

nextflu.org

Analytics can identify drifted clades (with drops in HI titers and signature genetic changes)


The frequencies of these clades can then be tracked through time
